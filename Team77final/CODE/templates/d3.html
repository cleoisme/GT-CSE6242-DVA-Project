SPONGE Stock Clustering

Stocks are typically clustered through categorical means, typically by sector. The SPONGE Algorithm provides a different way to cluster stocks, and potentially find investment opportunities. If you like a stock in a cluster you may want to purchase the other securities, as they are performing in tandem. You can select a date range below (formatted YYYYMMDD) to see what stocks belong to what clusters over a 25 year time period. Clicking on a node provides you with every stock in that cluster. Hover over the points to learn more about each single datapoint.
